Security

Your clips are
yours alone.

SnipSync uses end-to-end encryption powered by the same cryptography as Signal and Keybase. Your clips are encrypted on your device before they ever leave it. Not even we can read them.

  • Zero-knowledge We store encrypted blobs. We cannot decrypt your data, even if compelled to.
  • Vault password A separate password you set — never sent to our servers. Derives the encryption key locally.
  • XSalsa20-Poly1305 Military-grade symmetric encryption via NaCl. Per-clip random nonce — no two clips encrypt the same way.
  • 12-word recovery phrase Forgot your vault password? A recovery phrase lets you regain access to your encrypted clips.

How it works

Three keystrokes
from installed to syncing.

01

Download & sign in

Grab the app. Sign in with Google. That's your account — no forms, no confirmation emails, no nonsense.

02

Paste or type

Open SnipSync from your tray, paste whatever you need — text, links, or drag in a file — hit Enter. The clip is saved and synced instantly.

03

Open on any device

Walk to your other machine, click the tray icon. Your clip is already there. Copy it and keep moving.
