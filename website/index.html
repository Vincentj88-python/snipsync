Features

Built for the way
you actually work.

Mac
Windows

Real-time sync

Clips arrive on your other device before you can switch windows. Powered by Supabase Realtime over WebSockets.

Encrypted transport

TLS in transit, row-level security at rest. Your clips never touch a server you don't control.

Smart tagging

Links, code snippets, addresses, notes — auto-detected and filterable.

Instant search

Find that URL from three days ago. Live filtering across your entire clip history.

Tray-native

Lives in your menu bar. Click to open, click away to dismiss. Zero clutter.

Undo everything

Deleted a clip? 3-second undo window. Nothing is permanent until you say it is.

How it works

Three keystrokes
from installed to syncing.

01

Download & sign in

Grab the app. Sign in with Google. That's your account — no forms, no confirmation emails, no nonsense.

02

Paste or type

Open SnipSync from your tray, paste whatever you need, hit ⌘Enter. The clip is saved and synced instantly.

03

Open on any device

Walk to your other machine, click the tray icon. Your clip is already there. Copy it and keep moving.
